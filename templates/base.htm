What is this?

Password.ly is a super-simple tool to help you keep your password secure. Given one master password, we can generate a different password for every different site you visit.

Why is that important?

Security online is tricky. You can visit many different websites or internet cafes, and you're giving each of them access to every other site if you use the same password. Its similar to giving the owner of every shop you visit your house keys to drop the items off.
